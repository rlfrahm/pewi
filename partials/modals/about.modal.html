About

People in Ecosystems/Watershed Integration (PE/WI) is a simple learning tool designed to provide a scientific platform for teaching, discussing, and evaluating the trade-offs associated with agricultural land-use and management.

Project Team

Lisa Schulte Moore, Carrie Chennault, Ryan Frahm, Laura Roy, John Tyndall, & John VanDyk
Iowa State University, Department of Natural Resource Ecology & Management
